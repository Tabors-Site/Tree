This applies to **the seed** only: the code, Words, and store in [seed/](seed/) that define what a Story is, and the root Autographed Genesis Book. Nothing else in this repo is governed by it.

## The seed is the Word

The seed is the Word, the executable language a Story is spoken in. It is shared the way a book is shared in the **Library**. Because it is content addressed, a copy takes nothing from anyone and the original is undiminished. A fork is a **colophon**: a copy that carries its lineage back to the root. The AGPL's network copyleft is that library's law written as a legal term, so the Word you received free stays free for the next Name. May it stay **Ours**.

## Dual licensed

- **AGPL-3.0**: free and open, the default. Use, modify, fork, and distribute freely. The one obligation: if you run a modified seed over a network (hosted Stories, SaaS, APIs), make your modified source available to its users under the same license.
- **Commercial**: a paid option for organizations that cannot meet the AGPL terms. It lets you host as a service without the source availability obligation, build closed products on the seed, keep modifications private, or get priority support.

No code is held back either way. The commercial license grants the same seed source under different legal permissions, and the public seed stays AGPL-3.0 forever.
