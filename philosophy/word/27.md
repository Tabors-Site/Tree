## The base concept we are building toward

In Tabor's words: **generative** (a being reading its face and laying one Word at a time, the chain

This is 623/4's endpoint: the being is the head that reads the face and lays one word; the output is

## The three lanes from here — each on the built foundation, none from scratch

**A. The live generative loop — architected; prove it running. [medium]**
Every piece exists: runWordToStore stamps each word; the cognition is a SEE that emits the Word; the
response is a call-deed; "the moments re-invoke each other" is the loop (read face → word → stamp →
call → re-fold → next, resting on a SEE). What's missing is the *live demo* — a real being running the
loop end-to-end, the chain growing word by word, re-folding between. The deeper texture (one word per
moment: emit → re-fold → regenerate, vs. emit a multi-word Word then stamp) is the autoregressive form
(26.md, KV-cache = the fold). Build = wire the re-invocation loop + a live proof, not a rebuild.

**B. The keyboard / stamp-bar interface — the seed exists; the surface is a lane. [medium-large]**
The statement bar already does the core (verify-statement 6/6: a being types a Word → a real fact lands,
attributed). The 623/5+6 interface is the work: classify every key — word-key (build, IS) vs stamp-key
(seal, BE) — rename "spacebar" → **stamp bar**, "hit a stamp, the world reforms," and the honest
back-wall (only words that resolve to live ables reform the world; the rest shimmer / no-op). No app,
no menu — the keyboard IS the interface. A portal/UI lane on top of an engine that already makes a
typed word a fact.

**C. genesis `.word` faithful — the Word closes on itself. [largest, deepest, least started]**
The *ops* are `.word`s now. The *bootstrap* is not: word.word (the self-grounding root — "in the
beginning was the word"); do-makes-do at runtime (a being mints a new word onto the chain — the
vocabulary grows from inside); and the self-read `render(genesis → head) = "I AM THAT I AM"`. This is
9.md / 20.md — planned, not built — the self-hosting endpoint, 623/4's "it finished closing, the way a
song ends." The most work and the most aspirational; it is what makes genesis *faithful* — the same
Word top to bottom.

## The nearest demo — what to reach for first

**Type one word → it lands as a fact → and genesis is the same Word.** The statement-bar half is done
(verify-statement). The gap is making genesis itself a `.word` (lane C's word.word) so the system is the
Word top-to-bottom — then a person types one word, and it's a fact on the very chain genesis is written
on. That is the smallest end-to-end proof of the whole idea, and it is within reach: it needs lane C's
*first step* (genesis-as-Word), not the full generative loop or the full keyboard.
