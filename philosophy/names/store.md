localStore= where your own private resources are located

(Any unpublished models, code, ables, etc. all basically in a localStore as cas). This is the store of the whole tree. Every tree has one no matter what

Store = this is the global store, where you reference things from local store to put in. Accessible for others to reach as resources and use(copy over ibp).

Note: you could mainly be a “store” but everything your reality holds would be in localStore, all the resources that are in the store. The thing is that most of the resources in the localStore wouldn’t actually be “installed” or in use because they are focusing on hosting rather than applying.

So put simply, all resources end up in localStore. You can choose if others can have it by putting it in store. When you take from other stores it goes to your local store (and can decide if auto store too). Just because resources are in your local store doesn’t mean they are activated
