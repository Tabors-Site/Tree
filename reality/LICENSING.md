else in this repository is governed by it. Extensions, roleFlows,
clones/replicates (branches), portals (browsers), and work pulled or pushed to Horizon
are not covered here; they carry their own terms (or none).

The seed is dual licensed.

- **AGPL-3.0** (free and open). The default.
- **Commercial license** (paid). A secondary option for organizations

The commercial license does not withhold code. It grants the same seed
source under different legal permissions.

## Why two licenses

- Keeps the seed genuinely open and community friendly.
- Protects it from being taken proprietary without contributing back,
  since the AGPL network-copyleft applies to hosted derivatives.
- Creates a sustainable path for long-term development.
- Lets organizations that cannot adopt AGPL still use the seed legally.

## When the AGPL-3.0 is enough

The AGPL covers most users. You can use, modify, fork, and distribute
the seed freely. The one obligation: if you run a modified version over
a network (hosted realities, SaaS, APIs, anything network accessible),
you must make your modified seed source available to its users under
the same license.

Full text and the seed preamble: [seed/LICENSE](seed/LICENSE).

## When you want the commercial license

Organizations typically take the commercial license to:

- Host TreeOS realities as a service for their customers without the
  AGPL source-availability obligation.
- Build closed-source products or internal tools on top of the seed.
- Keep their seed modifications private.
- Get priority support, security patches, or custom development.

## How it works

- The public seed remains AGPL-3.0 forever.
- A commercial licensee receives the same seed source plus a separate
  agreement that supersedes the AGPL for their usage.
- No code is held back. The difference is the legal permissions.

## Scope, stated plainly

Only the seed is dual licensed. If you build an extension, author a
roleFlow, make a clone, write a portal, or publish to Horizon, that is
your own work under your own terms. The seed is the sovereign core;
the licensing here is about the seed and nothing more.
