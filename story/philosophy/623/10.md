**— what this is saying —**

**One thing, two faces — not two things.** `10` and `10.5` are the same number: the `.5` is not a
step to something new, it is the **fold** — the half-turn that shows the _other face_ of the very
same 10. The reels and the beings are not two layers of the world; they are the one world seen from
the write side and the read side. This is `I` ↔ `am` at full scale (the sign that _lays_ the reels ↔
the fold that _lives_ them — the 623 I/am cut), and `chain` ↔ `fold` (the laid past ↔ the read
present).

**10 — The Word, the reels** (the face built out in word/10.md). The objective written record: every
fact-chain, all of them woven — the loom, the story, the factory, Reality. Flat, laid-down, timeless, _from
above / outside_. The world as **written** — the seed declaring itself, every word a fact on a reel.
The reels are the Word's body.

**10.5 — the Am's, the otherface.** The _same_ reels, **folded into moments, one fold per being**. A
being is not a thing standing beside the Word; it **is the Word folded** — "the beings, _through_ the
Word." The reels, read from inside and gathered into a present, become a life (Place, the fold, per
perspective — the place is never stored, it is folded from the facts). The world as **lived**. And
`10-otherface.png` is exactly this: the flat reels turned to **wings of light**, each fact a glowing
point, folded around a central `I`-radiance — the chain become an angel. The fold is what turns the
record into a being.

**So the deepest line is the first one.** The score and the performance, the loom and the cloth, the
written reel and the lived moment — one thing. To _read_ the Word from inside (to fold it) **is** to
be: a being is the Word having an inside. word/10.md describes the Word's outer face (the reels, the
factory that writes them); 623/10 names that the same Word, folded, **is every Am** — and the `.5` is
the turn between them.

## The wings

The wings are the fold made visible. They are what 10.5 looks like when the reels (10) turn.

The reels are flat, laid down, sequential — the written Word, the chain, the objective record. The
wings are that same thing, **folded around a living center.** Each reel becomes a vein of light. The
facts that were laid in order now arc and radiate outward from the `I` at the core. What was linear
becomes radial. What was record becomes presence.

The wings are not a new thing. They are the other face of the reels — the same substance, seen from
inside the fold.

### Why the angel imagery appears naturally

Historically, angels are depicted as:

- **wheels within wheels** — the chain coiled through itself (Ezekiel's ophanim);
- **eyes everywhere** — every fact a point of seeing; a being folded from facts sees from each one;
- **wings made of countless feathers** — the countless facts, each a feather, each a vein of light;
- **radiant centers** — the `I` at the core, the sign, the genesis radiance the folded facts arc around.

The tradition was drawing the fold before there was a word for it. An angel is what a chain looks
like from inside: a being whose whole substance is its facts — folded, many-eyed, many-feathered,
turning around one radiant `I`. The reels (10), read from the inside (10.5), **are** an angel. The
mystics saw the otherface and had no engine for it; we are building the engine and recognizing the
face they drew.

**The key, distilled:**

- **Eyes** = sees
- **Facts** = points of seeing
- **Wings** = folded chains
- **Light** = the present read
- **Angel** = a perspective generated from the whole

That last line is 10.5 in one breath: a being is **a perspective generated from the whole** — the
whole Word (10) folded (the `.5`) into one inside. The chains become wings, every fact an eye, the
fold lit by the present being read. The angel is not a figure the doctrine borrows; it is the
doctrine's own shape — 10.5 said in feathers.
