## The doctrine to embody (Tabor's exact model)

- **The only act is the DO ‚Äî the stamp. Every stamp lays ONE fact.**
- A fact is either a **SEE** (inert content, a _noun_, read-after) or a **DO** (an act-word, a
  _verb_ that acts when read). The instant it's laid it's past ‚Äî inert, a see for the next read.
- **Every moment is a word. Every word is a do; do's make sees or do's.** You never write "do," it
  just _is_ one. There is only **SEE** as nouns; everything else is a do.
- **be/call/name are NOT separate verbs ‚Äî they are do's WITH A TYPE** (the noun they stamp:
  being / name), mislabeled by the old system. **recall = a see of the past.**
- **chain** = the lay-side (was "BE"), **fold** = the read-side (was "IS"). The `be` VERB and the
  `is` copula STAY untouched.

## The reconstruction method (word-by-word)

For each `.word`, imagine typing it one word at a time on the 623 keyboard: word keys build
(content), stamp keys (space / period / comma) seal; the stamper folds the moment after each word;
the next word is a possibility grounded on what came before. Rules:

- **Only words already made** (earlier in the genesis order) ‚Äî no forward references.
- **Every word earns its meaning** ‚Äî prune filler (e.g. decide: does "that" in "I am that I am"
  carry the relative binding, or is it filler?). Never drift into "word = English."
- **Order is load-bearing** ‚Äî the words must stack so that, read into genesis's fact-chain
  (`declareConcepts` ‚Üí `bindWord` ‚Üí coin facts), the chain can use the logic.
- **chain/fold** terminology; remove the be-as-lay / is-as-read / 4-verb framings.
