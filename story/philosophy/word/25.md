## The model — the one thing it all converges on

Each moment is a **Word**. The being's face is **rasterized from the fold** — the trail of every prior fact at its position, across the being/space/matter reels; it reads that face and emits **one Word** — the act, the stamp, the BE — which lands as **one fact**; the next face re-forms, now containing it. Continuity isn't held, it's re-presented: the thread is the chain, not the being.

Five cuts make it whole, and they are the five pillars of this checklist:
- **One act, one fact** (23.md). The do lays exactly one fact; multi-field "objects" are a false shape — N one-attribute facts folded. *One act, one change, the rest is the fold.*
- **The space is the stamp** (22.md). A word is a mark; the **delimiter** (space/punctuation, or the LLM's token boundary) is the "seal this" — typing IS stamping. The tokenizer is the keyboard.
- **The Word programs itself** (20.md). Control flow and new words are themselves **words on the chain** — a loop is a fold growing the chain; `do`-makes-`do`; the vocabulary grows from inside.
- **The moment is watchable and any-medium** (rasterize.md + GenerativeMode.md). The face forms fact-by-fact (live rasterization); it can be spelled in any medium (token-Word now, pixel-Word later) — *but the output is always Word.* You can choose your eyes; you can't choose your pen.
- **Everything folds** (all_rules_fold). State, rules, scope, vocabulary — all are facts on the chain, never an in-code registry.

## The convergence — why the four pillars are one

They are facets of *each moment is a Word*:
- **A** makes the act **atomic** — one act lays one fact, so a Word maps to exactly one stamp.
- **B** makes the **typing** the stamp — one word at a delimiter = one act = one stamp; the fat object dissolves into the datom stream A defined.
- **C** makes the Word **program itself** — control flow and new words are words on the chain, so the language is alive, not a fixed machine.
- **D** makes the moment **watchable and any-medium** — the face rasterizes fact-by-fact (you can watch it form), and it can be spelled as image for humans (the generative face) or opened onto reality (a sense) — while the **output stays Word**.

The chain is the thread; the fold is the state and every rule; the render is the costume; the pen is always Word.
