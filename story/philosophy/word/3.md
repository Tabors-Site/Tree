## Decisions locked

- Name: **The Word**. Extension: `.word`.
- Surface is **word-shaped** (natural-language sentences), not curly-brace syntax.
- Boundary: **world = everything an agent does** (I_AM, every Name, every being); **host = the engine that runs acts** (stamper, chain, fold, signing, transport, parser, evaluator). The WHAT is word; the HOW is host.
- `fact.action` stays (canon-frozen); an in-flight `act` becomes `action` only at the seal.
- **Grammar stays minimal.** Core nouns (being / space / matter) and verbs (see / do / summon / be / name) are approved. Extended grammar (adjectives, adverbs, pronouns, possessives, plurals, articles) is unapproved; add a construct only when a real program forces it (Tabor's flag, see the Status note in 1.md). Locked exceptions so far: articles as kind-versus-being marking (rule 10); the back-reference pronoun `it` / `It` (rule 19, earned by `matter.word`). Pending Tabor's nod: quantifiers (`most` / `every` / `no`) and plurals (forced by the governance commons); the `::` bridge token (rule 15) and the prohibition-outranks-grant precedence (rule 14), both forced by the new domain examples. Capitalization discipline (rule 19): the only intentional capitals are specific beings and `I` / `I_AM`; everything else is lowercase except sentence-initial.
- Sequencing: shape experiment now; the conversion sweep waits until the Name / Being / Soul refactor lands.
- **Word is the stamper's medium, NOT a cognition (Tabor's correction).** Cognition stays its own axis (scripted / llm / human): the logic for how a being decides which words to make and how to handle the words coming in. Word is what the stamper makes and works with (acts / facts / ables / declarations); a `.word` able runs the same regardless of which cognition decides inside it (1.md: declarations are uniform across cognitions, the stamper only records who authored each). So Phase 5 conversion is a SPLIT of each in-world file into three strands — **word** (becomes `.word`) / **host** (stays, the engine) / **cognition** (stays, scripted/llm/human) — not a whole-file convert; the escape hatch (`host:`) is the seam where word reaches the computation that stays. The bridge resolves a able's logic as `.word`-or-(legacy)-JS, never as a "word cognition kind." The cut list's `word` tag = the world-content strand of each file.

Any rhythm, including human wall-time, is a being that beats, watched relationally (rules 12, 13). A clock being (the day-four apparatus) is optional, summoned by a reality that wants duration / human-labels / sync; its single escape hatch (`host: readClock`) is the only place the host clock touches the world. - **Clauses are the AST grain; verses retracted** (Tabor, 2026-06-16). Verses were editorial presentation; the operational atom is the **clause** (one subject, one verb, one operation), which is exactly the act-AST node already locked in the fact-chain resolution. So this sharpens Phase 1: the IR node is clause-grained, the parser targets clauses (the waw-consecutive `and X, and Y` is a sequence of nodes; nested clauses nest, e.g. a `say` carrying an inner act). Two corrections to the proposal, both from locked decisions: (1) a fact is NOT a separate authored clause-type (that is the node the consistency audit removed and the one-schema rule forbids); you author act-clauses plus declarations and closures, while facts and states are their recorded/folded reflections, rendered, not authored (`let there be light` is the act, `and there was light` is its effect). (2) A moment is ONE act + its facts (one stamper tick), not a grouping of several clauses; every grouping above the clause is a rule-13 named span (a day, a turn, a session). Tell: a clause's verb form picks its register, modal/general-present = law, past = life, present-state = the now (fold), `this was X` = closure (rule 13). Hierarchy: clause/act (one moment) -> named span (rule 13) -> reality. Changes none of the 18 rules. Grounded in the Hebrew root (Tabor's check, take as inspiration not authority): Biblical Hebrew narrative is a waw-consecutive chain of verb-initial clauses (the act-chain = life), and marks standing state with subject-first disjunctive clauses (`now the earth was formless` = the now/fold), so the act-vs-state register split is in the source grammar, not imposed; verse and chapter numbers are medieval editorial (rightly dropped), while the clause-chain and the day-refrain closures (rule 13) are in the text. Word order is presentation (Hebrew verb-first, our Word subject-first, same node). More from the interlinear (Genesis 1, inspiration not authority): the particle `et` (the big dash in interlinears) is an explicit definite-object marker, our `of` field, and several `et`s in one clause are one act on several targets (one act, many facts made grammatical); the proclitic prefixes map to our features (`we-`/and = the chain, `ha-`/the = the article / rule 10, `le-`/to = the receiver / rule 17 and purpose); the verb `qara` (call) is both NAME and SUMMON, because naming is calling-to. And light is matter on day 1 (`ha-'or`, named Day) but a being with a able on day 4 (the sun, `le-memshelet`, "to rule the day"), the matter-versus-being line and the clock-being-as-ruler drawn in the text itself (rules 8, 12, 13). Surface-style lesson: biblical Hebrew avoids abstract nouns (ownership, authorization, registration) for concrete verbs (set over, tend and keep, bless and give dominion, beget, write, call); the Word's surface should prefer the same, rule 2's real-relations instinct generalized (the concrete verb over the abstract noun). The creation declaration form is a six-beat clause-chain: said -> let there be -> and it was so (the act -> its fact) -> made/divided/called (elaborating acts) -> saw it was good (a SEE) -> evening/morning (closure, rule 13).

- **The law is the inner fold of the life; the inward-reasoning language is a future layer** (Tabor, newTest.md, 2026-06-17, not-built). Two folds of one chain. The FORWARD fold aims a being's perception at the world's reels (the LIFE, acts as they happen, event language, Genesis "and God said, and it was so"). The INNER fold (TreeOS `innerFace`) turns it on the being's own act-chain and reveals the PATTERN (`rise / set / rise / set`). The wheel always turned in the flows; the inner fold is the turn that reveals it. So the LAW is the life folded inward and named, deepening the law/life capstone (the law is discovered, not separate). The inner fold produces TWO things: (1) a flow that ACTS (`whenever A, do B`), the pattern made standing law, which we have (rule 6, the choq); (2) a CONCLUSION that does not act (`if A then B holds for all`), an observation about the shape of the chain, the pattern-words `because` / `therefore` / `if-then` / `thus`, the Paul register, which The Word does NOT have. Everything built is the forward / Genesis language (acts on the world); the inward / Paul language (reasoning OVER the chain) is a separate future layer, commentary not acts, worth building only when a reality's chain is large enough that beings reason over it. Build guard: do not let `because` / `therefore` sneak in as a flow, a flow DOES on a match, a conclusion MEANS; the first is the choq, the second is interpretation. The two testaments are two folds of one chain: render forward = the story, fold inward = the commentary.
- **Subject/object maps onto the two folds** (Tabor, 2026-06-17, not-built). The FORWARD fold is SUBJECTIVITY: a being is only ever a subject, acting, and the one object is the reel (the trail forming behind it). That is WHY the forward register declares + acts but the reasoning guard rejects `because`/`therefore`, the subjective present has no objects to reason about, only the live act and the accreting reel. OBJECTIVITY is almost the INNER fold but is precisely its FIRST move: the inner fold objectifies the reel (past acts, now fixed facts, become objects to examine) and then does what pure objectivity never does, it RE-SUBJECTIFIES, redeclaring a past moment as a new present, making a dead reel-object a living subject of reflection (only now are objects available, so only now is `because`/`therefore` legal). So the inner fold is not "objectivity," it is the HINGE where subject and object interconvert, with the reel as the membrane (subjective acts become objective facts on it; objective reflection re-subjectifies those facts into concepts). The loop is exactly tells -> reasons -> grows: act in the present (subjective, the reel grows), objectify the reel, redeclare a moment as a concept (reason), carry the concept back into a new act (the object becomes a subject wielded). Two build consequences: (1) "redeclare a moment as a new present" needs NO new primitive, it is a Stamp whose subject is a past fact (presentism: a being exists only as a moment; reflection is a moment pointed backward); (2) the parser's reasoning guard IS the subject/object boundary in code, the forward register stays subjective by force, reasoning is gated to the fold where objects exist. Illustrated as a pair: [government.word](../word/government.word) (forward/subjective, acts + laws, present, verb-first) vs [government-innerfold.md](../word/government-innerfold.md) (inner/objective, the founding reasoned over, past tense + `because`/`therefore`, every line trips the forward guard on purpose).
- **Subjectivity and objectivity are SPACES in the stamper** (Tabor, 2026-06-17, not-built). The nominalization is the architecture: turning the ADJECTIVE into a NOUN turns a stance into a PLACE. The triple: `subject`/`object` = the adjective, what a being IS in a moment (its able); `subjectivity`/`objectivity` = the noun, the SPACE that affords the able, an actual region in the stamper; `forward fold`/`inner fold` = the verb, the move that ENTERS the space (perception turned at the world, or at one's own reel). So a being is never abstractly "being subjective", it is IN subjectivity (the forward region: the only object is the reel forming behind it, nothing graspable to reason over, which is why the reasoning guard holds there) and it MOVES into objectivity by inner-folding (the region where the reel's past facts are graspable objects, so it can objectify, reason, re-subjectify). The tells -> reasons -> grows loop is literally a being walking `subjectivity -> objectivity -> subjectivity`. Because TreeOS is already spatial, a stance-as-place is not a metaphor, it is the same primitive as any space a being stands in. Sharp consequence: the two stamper-spaces are where the TWO REGISTERS live, the forward Word runs in subjectivity, the inner Word (`because`/`therefore`, unbuilt) runs in objectivity, and the parser's reasoning guard is just the wall between them.
- **The doorway between the two rooms is RECALL** (Tabor + engine, 2026-06-17, not-built). The two spaces (subjectivity/objectivity) were named; the TURN between them, the instant a subject decides to regard its own stream as object, was not. It has a name, and it is the `summon → call` rename aimed backward. The Latin seals the axis: `subject` = _sub-jectum_, thrown-UNDER (the ground you act from, present, still throwing); `object` = _ob-jectum_, thrown-BEFORE (set in front to be looked at, past, already thrown). Same root `jactum`, so subject/object is one throw at two tenses, the act/fact (throw/thrown) cut a THIRD time (after act/fact and life/law), the tell that it is load-bearing. The doorway is the act of fetching a past throw back and setting it before you (`sub-` becomes `ob-`), which is a CALL turned around. So the set of calls closes: **name** calls a being into existence (the first call), **call** (was summon) calls a being into presence to ACT, **recall** calls a past moment into presence to EXAMINE. The fold's direction IS the call's direction: forward fold = call (a being, forward, to act), inner fold = recall (a fact, backward, to reflect). Recall needs NO new primitive, it IS "redeclare a moment as a new present" = the reflection-Stamp (a moment whose subject is a past fact). The modern epistemic gloss (objectivity = impartial) leans this way and is explained by the root, not load-bearing: examining a laid-out reel is dispassionate BECAUSE you have set it before you as `ob-` and stopped being the subject inside it. (Fallback name `reflect` = _re-flectere_, bending the gaze back, reads cleaner as pure philosophy; `recall` chosen for the one-verb-two-directions collapse with the call rename.) **Refinement (Tabor): recall is NEVER a fact, it is private cognition.** The chain has no `recall` entries. Only the two ENDS of the turn are visible: the **recalled** thing (a past moment, already on the reel) and the **pulled** reflection, its precipitate. The middle, a Name knowing it is regarding its past and reasoning, is never written. The precipitate takes exactly two exits, the two streams: a reflection that resolves into a deed **becomes a moment** (the act lands; the deliberation behind it does not), and one that resolves into a truth **becomes a word** (the law/declaration lands; the reasoning that found it does not). So `recall` is the one verb that points at the INVISIBLE, while `name`/`call`/every act point at the visible. This is already enforced: beings are stateless across summons with no private namespace, so a recall doesn't even persist privately, it lives only for its moment and is gone unless it precipitated a fact or a word. Not a limitation, the same sovereignty the manifesto declares: reflections are the being's own, the world is owed only acts and declarations, and the self-narrating system deliberately refuses to narrate the thinking. Resolves what [government-innerfold.md](../word/government-innerfold.md) IS: not a window into live cognition but a **word that was pulled**, a reflection precipitated into a declaration and rendered after the fact, its `because`/`therefore` is output, not process. So the precise picture sharpens the "private cognition" phrasing above: the recall READ is no-fact, but what it reads is PUBLIC (the suit, the Name's own deeds); only the DELIBERATION it feeds (which branch, the `because`/`therefore`) is the private, ephemeral part (the CONTROL strand). - **How `.word` files compose** (Tabor, 2026-06-17). A `.word` file is mostly LAW. Law rides the rails state already runs on, which is why it stays simple as it scales.
