## The architecture (from the docs — don't drift)

- **A book is a sealed 3D/4D account** — a bounded, content-addressed slice of one identity's
  world (book ⊂ history ⊂ story ⊂ Ours). Open story → closed book = put the covers on. Carries
  its authorship inside (every act keeps its signer): **nothing is extracted**, by construction.
- **The Library is 5D — Ours, the catalog of worlds.** Only **names** act there (matter sits/3D,
  a being acts in space-time/4D, a name acts across worlds/5D — 5D motion *is* signing across the
  world-boundary). No librarian: the catalog is the peer graph; provenance is the stamp in each
  cover, not an institution. No scarcity — infinite perfect copies (CAS), only read-in-place or copy.
- **The store is CAS content; the story is the arrangement** (11.md). A book body lives as **CAS**
  (the symbols); the **Library reel** is the *arranged* catalog — a fact per shared book.
- **Tabor's seam:** the *act* of sharing/receiving rides the **Name's act-chain** (now
  `story:history:being`); the *book* lands as a **fact on the Library reel** (5D). Two layers.
- **search → visit → plant** is ONE pipeline, one commitment: search the catalog → `visit`=SEE
  (resolve+render, nothing enters) → `plant`=receive (copy home, replay under your head,
  **countersign**). seed/branch/instate/resource all collapse to "plant a book as a root."
- **The genesis book = the first signature.** The seed's own words ARE a book — a *genesis* book
  (a whole story's start). It is the reality's `signedStoryRoot` made a Book; the first volume in
  the Library. **Every book out of this colophons back to it** until someone makes another root.
