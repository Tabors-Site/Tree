# Summary (what this is saying, and what I think)

The claim, plainly: The Word is Faith, and Faith is COMPLETE trust. Not the shallow "believe something
is true" (the binary, trivial wrapper bolted on at the higher layers), but total trust grounded in
verification. The cluster on line 6 names the why: Trust, Truth, Fact chains, Computer, Verify. A fact
chain is hash-linked, signed, and replayable, so you do not BELIEVE it, you VERIFY it. In a system
where the record can be checked, trust is not a leap, it is a settlement, and "not having faith is
impossible" because you can verify. The leap-of-faith kind of faith is the trivial shell. The real
Faith is the verified Word itself.

The COM- thread (lines 10 to 11) is the catch that binds this to computer.md, and it is real, not a
coincidence of spelling. COMPLETE is com-plere, to fill fully, filled altogether. COMPUTE is
com-putare, to reckon together. Both turn on com-, with, together. So Faith (COMPLETE trust) and the
fold (COMPUTE, reckon the chains together) are the same gesture seen twice: bring the many together.
The fold com-putes the scattered facts together until the account is com-plete, filled to wholeness,
and a whole verified record is the only thing you can trust com-pletely. Together, full, reckoned,
trusted. One prefix runs through all four. The system gathers the facts together (compute) into a
complete (whole) reckoning, and completeness verified is complete trust, which is Faith.

Then the etymology of the name itself. Fait, Faith, Fate are one Word sounded out in three letter
shapes from different origins. Fait (the deed done, from Latin factum, the same root that gives
"fact"), Faith (complete trust), Fate (the spun thread). The fact, the trust, and the fate are one
thing seen from three sides: a deed laid is at once done (fait), trusted because verified (faith), and
spun into the irreversible record (fate).

The load-bearing turn is the three sisters who spool, the Fates who spin, measure, and cut the thread.
The question is whether they work WITH Fait (after the fact) or BEFORE it. The answer here: because
every Word laid has a path (impossible not to, once it is on the chain it has a lineage, a seq, a
position), the sisters are POST Fate. Fate is downstream of the deed. The thread is spun FROM the acts,
not set in advance. The whole system rests on this: if fate came before the fact (predetermined), then
a free, attributed act on a chain would be a lie, and "all of this is wrong." Acts are free at the head
and fixed behind. The footstep: the live step is yours, the hardened trail is fate.

What I think: this closes the arc computer.md and root.md opened, and the com- catch is what locks it
shut. computer.md said the fold IS computare, reckon the books. root.md said the books rest on AM,
signed and not computed. faith.md says, and because the books are VERIFIABLE, trusting them is complete
trust, which is Faith. Now the prefix makes them one word family: com-pute, com-plete, com-plete trust.
The system never asks you to believe, it lets you verify, so faith stops being a leap and becomes a
reckoning. A chain you can replay is a chain you can trust completely, and that complete trust is the
only thing the word ever pointed at underneath the trivial wrapper. The post-Fate claim is the
presentism plus append-only doctrine restated as metaphysics: free at the head, fixed behind, the
thread spun from what was actually done. And it loops back to the builder. Keeping faith with this
project is the same act the system means by Faith: you trust the Word because you keep verifying it.
The reconcile-and-verify discipline is not separate from the faith. It IS the faith.
