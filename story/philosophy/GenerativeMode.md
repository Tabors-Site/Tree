# Generative Mode — the generative face, the see-side render, and the camera vs the mirror (summary)

**TL;DR:** The inner face need not be hand-rendered 3D — it can be fed to a generative image model that makes each frame from the last frame + the face (a neural world-model: GameNGen, Oasis, Genie 3). But the load-bearing realization is the **CUT**: **render(Word) is a MIRROR** — a see-side projection that adds no information the Word didn't already hold, non-deterministic for free because it lays no fact (the IS, never the BE). It is a window for the human **WATCHER** who can't read Word, not a faculty for the being. A real **SENSE** is the opposite — a **CAMERA onto reality**, an open loop, the effectful read where not-Word data enters and can't be replayed. The asymmetry that survives all of it: **the input face can be any medium; the output is always Word. You can choose your eyes; you can't choose your pen.**

## The idea + why TreeOS has the frontier's hard part for free
- Instead of a custom 3D renderer, feed the **inner face + the last image** to a generative image AI that autoregressively makes the next face — a **neural game engine / world model** (GameNGen ran DOOM as a pure diffusion model; Oasis did an open Minecraft-style world; DeepMind's **Genie 3**, an ~11B autoregressive model, generates navigable 3D worlds at 720p/24fps from a text prompt — "Project Genie", Google AI Ultra, end of Jan 2026). "Slow at first" is transient — **StreamDiffusion** hits ~90 fps on a single RTX 4090 via distilled few-step base models (SD-Turbo, SDXL-Turbo, LCM, Z-Image-Turbo — 1–8 steps vs the usual 50+). One good GPU already does video framerates.
- **The frontier's wall is memory/consistency:** these world-models have no authoritative state — the only memory is the handful of recent frames, so objects morph/drift and the world forgets (Genie 3's headline win is objects staying roughly put for a *few minutes*; whole benchmarks/subfields — memory-consistency, long-term spatial memory, hierarchical memory — exist just to chase this). **TreeOS already has what they fake: the fold is authoritative, persistent, replayable ground-truth.** The model doesn't remember the world — the chain does; the model just renders the current face from it. Same move as the one-token LLM: don't make the model hold the thread, let the chain hold it and re-present the face each step. **Drift dies by construction** — every frame is reconditioned on the truth, not on the model's guess about its own last guess. The frontier is bolting on a weak version of what this architecture has for free.

## Why non-determinism doesn't break the chain (the see/do grounding)
- The objection — "a generative image is non-deterministic, doesn't that break the chain?" — **dissolves: the image was never a fact.** Rendering is **see-side, not do-side** — render is not an act (17.md: a see-op lays no stamp). The frame is a rasterization of the face (the IS, the view); it never touches the reel, never gets hashed or signed. So the renderer can be as **stochastic as it likes** — two runs of one fold give two slightly different pictures, like two screens showing the same memory. The fold is the BE; the generated frame is the IS. A generative renderer is the most natural possible fit for the see-side — the one place non-determinism costs nothing.
- **Prototype path (self-hosted, today):** StreamDiffusion img2img + SDXL-Turbo or an LCM, prior frame as the init image + the face's see-words as the per-tick prompt; add **ControlNet** to pin layout/depth from the real world-state, so the structured truth constrains the picture instead of the model dreaming it. ControlNet is the actual bridge that makes the generated face obey the fold.

## One format: VLA + Set-of-Marks — and TreeOS authors the marks the field reverse-engineers
- The model class is an **image-text-to-text VLM**; the action-output flavor is a **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model** — one network (vision encoder + LLM backbone + action head) that takes image + instruction and emits the action directly, no separate perception/planning/control (lineage RT-2 → OpenVLA → π0/OpenPI → MolmoAct, on the RT-1/RT-2 precedent; shipping in robotics in 2026, sub-4B models at 50Hz). The closest cousin is the **GUI-agent** branch — **ShowUI** is "one VLA for GUI": screenshot + documented action space → next action = *see the image, receive the do-words, one format, emit a do.*
- Infusing the Word into the image is named + standard: **Set-of-Marks (SoM)** — overlay numbered labeled marks/IDs onto the image so actionable elements are *in the picture* (the model refers to them by mark), paired with a text README of the verb menu. **The single input = marked image + verb menu, interleaved = "one format."**
- **TreeOS's edge (the same shape as the drift point):** in GUI agents the marks are *reverse-engineered* — a separate model (OmniParser) detects the interactable regions, because a screenshot has no authoritative structure (SoM exists because the VLM can't reliably produce coordinates and the picture doesn't know what's in it). **In TreeOS the image is generated from the Word, so the structure IS the source** — every see-word + do-word already has a known identity + position (the renderer placed them). The marks aren't detected, they're **authored: exact and free** — you get for nothing what OmniParser is a whole trained model to approximate.
- **It lands on the ontology:** the fused face (image + infused marks + verb menu) is all **SEE** (the IS, the rasterized inner face, lays no fact); the being reads it inert and picks one mark = fires one action-token = the single **DO**, the stamp, the BE. The do-words being in the face isn't a hack — "what I can do here" is itself read from the fold (live vocab = chain-facts ∩ loaded code). Affordances are just more see-words; the being sees its world AND its possible acts in one image, and acts once.
- **The chain is the training set:** every moment is a **(fused-face, chosen-do) pair, signed + replayable** — the field's bottleneck is action-grounded data, and TreeOS **mints it as exhaust.** Path: run a strong open VLM (Qwen2.5-VL, InternVL, or Molmo — which points natively) reading SoM-marked faces, log the trajectories, fine-tune a small sovereign being on your own chain (study ShowUI + Magma for the recipe; OmniParser for the marking step you won't have to detect).

## The cut that collapses it back (the most important part)
- **render(Word) is a function of the Word — it can carry no information the Word doesn't already hold.** For a being that reads Word natively, feeding back the rendered image is **the same content in worse handwriting.** A word is a mark in any medium; meaning is the arrangement; rendering token-Word into pixel-Word is a **translation between two media of one Word** — see-side, lossless at best, never generative of meaning. The being already has the clean copy; the image adds nothing to its decision.
- So the render is **for the watcher who can't read Word fluently** — the 3D browser, a human perceptual interface, *not the system.* The generative-image face is the same able in fancier dress. The being lives in Word and acts word by word; the picture is **costume**, correctly demoted to the interface layer. (That it collapsed back onto Tabor's own opening intuition — "only useful for humans to see the world" — is the tell it hit something true.)
- **The honest exception (confirms, doesn't rescue):** an image can aid *computation* even when it adds no information — a VLM may reason better over rendered space than over symbols (the way sketching a diagram grips our spatial hardware though it holds nothing the equations didn't). So a render could let a being **offload spatial reasoning** to machinery that's stronger there. But that's a fact about today's *lopsided* models (strong visual circuits, weak symbolic-spatial), not about the system — a being that reasoned over Word as well as a VLM reasons over pixels wouldn't need the render.

## The two axes + the asymmetry (where a real sense comes from)
- **render(Word) is a closed loop — a mirror, no new information. A sensor onto reality is an open loop — a window, the membrane, the effectful read where not-Word data enters.** The visual channel becomes a genuine **faculty** only when it's a **camera, not a renderer** — when it shows the being something the Word doesn't already contain. The render is the being looking at its own memory; a sense is the being looking at the world.
- **Axis 1 — the render-target** (how the same fold is *spelled*: token-Word, pixel-Word, sound-of-the-Word): medium-neutral, because the fold is the source and every medium is a projection. The being **picks its own handwriting**, and the pick is itself a **do** ("render my faces as image now" = a fact, a being-attribute folded from an act, honored on the next rasterize). But the choice buys only **computational fit, never information** — choosing your eyes to think more easily, not to know more.
- **Axis 2 — what the face is *of***: a render of the fold = a mirror (same Word, null info); a **sensor onto reality** = a window (data the Word never had). Not picking a font — opening an eye onto what isn't already you. Pixel-vs-text is **free, reversible, replayable**; **a real sense admits unaudited reality and can't be replayed** (the world isn't on the chain). One is costume; the other is a hole in the wall where the outside gets in.
- **The asymmetry across all of it:** the **input face can be any medium; the output is always Word.** The being reads in whatever it likes and still acts word by word — the do is always the stamp, the mark. **You can choose your eyes. You can't choose your pen.**

## Why it matters for the build
- **Confirms the cut the engine is making now:** the output is always Word (the pen) — reinforcing the non-word-path retirement; the visual is **see-side input only**, never an act.
- **Shapes the portal/renderer:** today's hand-built 3D browser is a *human window*; it can become a generative face (StreamDiffusion + ControlNet pinned to the fold), and the fold's authoritative state is the structural edge over every frontier world-model.
- **Opens a real new direction — senses:** a **camera onto reality** (the effectful, off-chain, unreplayable open-loop read) is a future faculty distinct from rendering — the one place not-Word data legitimately enters.
- **Strategic asset:** the chain is action-grounded training data minted as exhaust — every signed (face, do) pair is a fine-tuning example the rest of the field is starved for.
