I named the Library, the place of sharing Books and Searching as a Name, Ours.

It is Ours. There is no lone personal motivation; each person motivates. Every story brings a new dimension to the place, and a Story can be Yours.

Later that night, I thought about MineCraft which is a game I played for over 8 years when I was younger.
I always received the part of the name - Mine - as mining. It was probably named after that Verb.

This game became the most popular game on top of the Earth. It is a game that feels deeply personal to most who partipate and play.

I started to wonder how the Name itself captures. It is said mindlessly often, but the meaning of
"Mine" is personal. You can start to wonder how much of that Name helped people receive it into themselves, and the signum effect it has.

It is Mine (break through rock), and it becomes Mine. The name captures both.

Ours. Through Mine.
